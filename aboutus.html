About Qantas PTFS

Discover our mission, vision, and the passionate community behind Qantas PTFS, your premier virtual airline experience.

Our Story

Qantas PTFS was founded to provide pilots and crew with an immersive, professional, and fun flying experience. We focus on realistic operations, engaging community events, and creating a welcoming environment for all skill levels. Our team is passionate about aviation and aims to bring the heart of Australia to the skies of PTFS.


Mission

To deliver the most engaging, realistic, and community-driven virtual airline experience, inspiring pilots and crew to grow together and enjoy every flight.


Vision

To become the leading virtual airline in PTFS, known for our professionalism, community, and memorable flying adventures.


Community

Our community is the backbone of Qantas PTFS. We encourage collaboration, skill-building, and friendship through group flights, events, and a friendly Discord server.
